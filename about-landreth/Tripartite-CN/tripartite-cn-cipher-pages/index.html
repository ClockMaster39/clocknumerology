How Was The "Tripartite-CN" Cipher Made?

Tripartite-CN was created using three (ie. Tripartite) ciphers of my 'clock numerology' collection: 'Clock Numerology; 2clock2; & 25th-Hour'. The completed cipher was derived from adding those three ciphers together.

About The "Tripartite-CN" Cipher

"All of my clock numerology ciphers were inspired by the mechanics of time. It is important to know that this cipher is mirrored from letters 'N' through 'Z'. For example, 'N=78', 'O=19', 'P=68', 'Q=21', etc etc."

-Chris Landreth-
