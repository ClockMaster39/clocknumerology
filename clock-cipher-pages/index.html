How Was The "Clock Numerology" Cipher Made?

I do not remember 'exactly' how I got the numbers to this cipher. First I assigned a temporary value of 3600 (ie. "60 x 60 x 1" from 60sec:60min:1hr/per day) to the letter 'A'. I then multiplied 3 x 6 (not counting zeroes) and finally (reduced?). For letter 'B' I gave a temp. value of 216000 (ie. 3600 x 60) and so on through letter 'M'. An intersting thing I did for the last calculation was that I (think?) I added '1' to the first half (or just letter 'A & Z') of the alphabet, and then subtracted '1' from the last half (or just letter 'B & Y') of the alphabet -thereby symbolising Day/Night (AM & PM).

About The "Clock Numerology" Cipher

"All of my clock numerology ciphers were inspired by the mechanics of time. It is important to know that this cipher is mirrored from letters 'N' through 'Z'. For example, 'N=13', 'O=10', 'P=7', 'Q=7', etc etc."
